A laptop, also known as a laptop, is a type of personal computer that is absolutely minimalist to help users easily move and carry with them.

Definition of laptop

Desktop computers are certainly a device that is very familiar to us in today's modern life. A desktop computer includes two main components: the CPU case and the screen. However, desktop computers have a fatal weakness: they are not mobile and users can only stay in one place. This is really an extremely inconvenient thing in today's constantly moving society. And so, laptops (laptops) were born, meeting the needs of mobility while still ensuring what desktop computers can do.

Instead of running directly on a power source, this device has a built-in or built-in battery that allows the laptop to operate even when there is no direct power supply. In addition, laptops are also integrated with necessary functions and features that users need such as: keyboard, mouse, speakers, optical drive, various connection ports, wireless connection, etc.

As we all know, Apple is not only famous for its iPhone smartphones, but MacBook laptops running MacOS are also considered its spearhead.
